Welcome to Tallaght University Hospital

Tallaght university Hospital uses the Mancherster triage system to categorise patients.
Patients in category one and two will be seen immediately as their condition is said to be critical.
Patients in categories three, four and five are not in a critical condition and will be seen as soon as possible.
